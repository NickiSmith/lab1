Urban Birdwatching on Global Big Day

5 May 2018

Each year, thousands of birdwatchers participate in eBird's annual birdwatching event. Many of these birders live, and birdwatch, in cities. While the exact number of observations reported within large cities may come as a surprise, the time of day urban birdwatching gets under way may not be...

Do the early birds really get the worm? See what time 2018 Global Big Day's urban birdwatchers began tracking their observations in the East Coast's most populous cities!
